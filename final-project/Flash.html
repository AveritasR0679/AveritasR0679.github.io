Adobe Animate

         Adobe Animate is an animation and multimedia authoring tool that creates media for multiple platforms and playback technologies. Knowing where your final animation will play determines what type of document you'll choose as a file. For the examples below, I originally created them with ActionScript 3, which was simple and fun; however, owing to some unknown circumstances, I was forced to convert them to HTML5 format.

         In Adobe Animate, we have something called a playback environment, the technology that your final, published files use to play. Your animation could play in a Flash Player, or could play in a browser with the Flash Player, or they could be play in a browser with HTML5 and JavaScript. They could also be played on mobile devices, such as your cell phones and tablets.

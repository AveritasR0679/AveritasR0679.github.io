Adobe Illustrator

         Adobe Illustrator is the industry-standard illustration application for print, multimedia, and online graphics. Whether you are a designer or a technical illustrator producing artwork for print publishing, an artist producing multimedia graphics, or a creator of web pages or online content, Adobe Illustrator offers you the tools you need to get professional-quality results.

         In Adobe Illustrator, we commonly work with vector graphics - graphical representations of mathematical objects such as lines, curves, polygons and whatsoever. In other words, vectors are not like bitmaps which consist of limited amount of pixels - when you zoom in, the image gets blurry; conversely, vectors are made up of paths that are not restricted by pixels. As a result, the graphics will not become pixelated even when you zoom in.
